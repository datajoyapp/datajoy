About Us

Our Technology

We provide a special purpose DNS server which is regularly updated with a blacklist of adservers. This DNS server sits behind a high performance proxy server to provide great performance.

Our Process

For every website you visit, your computer or phone sends us a type of request called a DNS lookup request. We compare that URL with our masterlist of known ad servers. If we don't find it there, we respond with a thumbs up meaning your computer can go ahead to that URL. Otherwise, we tell your computer to STOP.

Our Special Sauce

We are the first publicly accessible adblocker which can work at the network level. You can block ads not just your personal device but for ALL devices on your Wifi with one minor setting change. No other publicly available adblocker has this capability.

We also save you cellular data which lets you surf more. How cool is that?!?

Shut Up and Take My Money!

Earning a living off the internet is hard. We understand that. And us blocking ads doesn't help you..at all. Therefore, we are trialing a way to pay content creators for their work.

Here is what YOU need to do...

  • Step 1
    1. Go to this SHA256 calculator
    2. Enter your domain name (lower case only) in the data field. If you own a subdomain like abc.blogspot.com, enter that instead
    3. Click the "Calculate SHA256 Hash" button
      • E.g., for datajoy.us, the SHA256 Hash is f41372769d856f3516fc6a09f779c5a4112d1ea5c7022f26a898e3a28f814509
      • E.g., for abc.blogspot.com, the SHA256 Hash is 589b2cb27949977d6dcac044b537f754660cc94abed89a9046c474c3715806a4
    4. Create an HTML page whose name matches the results of Step #1.3
      • E.g., for Datajoy.us, the HTML page name is f41372769d856f3516fc6a09f779c5a4112d1ea5c7022f26a898e3a28f814509.html
      • E.g., for abc.blogspot.com, the HTML page name will be 589b2cb27949977d6dcac044b537f754660cc94abed89a9046c474c3715806a4.html
  • Step 2: Put an email address you control as the ONLY content of that HTML page
  • Step 3: Make that HTML page accessible to the public. E.g., see our page
  • On the first of every month, based on how many of our users went to your domain, we will PayPal you 1c/visit at that email address

Welcome to Datajoy

We block all ads on your personal mobile devices on wifi and can do the same for your entire wifi network. You can use us to block ads on most Android™ devices. Soon, you will be able to block ads on your iDevices too.

We are great for families with lots of devices or with parents living elsewhere whose devices you want to protect. We can be used at coffee shops, small offices, schools, or even AirBnBs who want to give their users a good internet experience.

We work both on browsers and apps. Inside apps, we can block ads a majority of the time though they slip through the cracks on YouTube and Spotify. We are working on a fix for this. If you want to know more, talk to us through the Contact page.

About Us

Our Technology

We are built on top of the very amazing pi-hole. Please consider supporting pi-hole for the amazing work they do to keep the internet free.

Our Process

For every website you visit, your computer or phone sends us a type of request called a DNS lookup request. We compare that URL with our masterlist of known ad servers. If we don't find it there, we respond with a thumbs up meaning your computer can go ahead to that URL. Otherwise, we tell your computer to STOP.

Our Special Sauce

We are the first adblocker which can work at the network level. You can block ads not just your personal device but for ALL devices on your Wifi with one minor setting change. No other publicly available adblocker has this capability.

TL;DR - we only have one bit of data about you (which we don't keep for long*)

Privacy Policy

Your trust is critical to us and we understand that earning and keeping your trust is a daily endevor. We commit to discussing any changes to this privacy policy with you, our user community.

  • We are committed to full transparency
  • Apart from your IP address, we don't have any personal information about you
  • We do not lease or sell any data to anyone
  • We intend to comply with any lawful request for our logs made by law enforcement
  • We do log your DNS requests which contains your IP address. These logs are discarded within 2 days of collection. In future, we may wish to store logs to serve our monetization strategy. We commit to only storing fully depersonalized logs

If you have ANY questions about our privacy and logging policies, please contact us through the channels mentioned in the Contact page.

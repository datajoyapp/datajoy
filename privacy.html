Privacy and Logging Policy

TL;DR - we only have one bit of data about you (which we don't keep for long*)

Privacy Policy

Your trust is critical to us and we understand that earning and keeping your trust is a daily endevor. We commit to discussing any changes to this privacy policy with you, our user community.

  • We are committed to full transparency
  • Apart from your IP address, we don't have any personal information about you
  • We do not lease or sell any data to anyone
  • We intend to comply with any lawful request for our logs made by law enforcement
  • We do log your DNS requests which contains your IP address. These logs are discarded within 2 days of collection. In future, we may wish to store logs to serve our monetization strategy. We commit to only storing fully depersonalized logs

If you have ANY questions about our privacy and logging policies, please contact us through the channels mentioned in the Contact page.
